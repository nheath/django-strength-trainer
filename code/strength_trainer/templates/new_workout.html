The 5/3/1 workout focuses on what many would call the core lifts. All that is needed is your to construct your personalized plan is your 1 rep max for each of the core lifts.
1 rep max = the most weight that right now you could lift.


More about the workout
Developed by Jim Wendler. This program was designed to increase functional strength gains that could be sustained over a long period of time. Inspired after years of powerlifting Jim realized he was strong but not very functional. His passion to continue to lift but his desire to have functional strength lead to 5-3-1.New lifters looks at a program like this and think its too "slow" or "light. But it is a slow progressive approach that makes this program something you can do for years and continue to get results.
