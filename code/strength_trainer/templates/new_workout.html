The 5/3/1 workout focuses on what many would call the core lifts. All that is needed is your 1 rep max for each of these workouts. This is the most weight that right now you could lift.


A little more about the workout, it was developed by Jim Wendler. He designed this program to produce functional strength gains that could be sustained over a long period of time. Many new lifters looks at a program like this and think its too "slow" or "light. But it is a slow progressive approach that makes this program something you can do for years and continue to get results.
